Safety Notice

If you're in immediate danger, please call 100 immediately. This platform is for non-emergency reporting and connecting with local police stations and lawyers in India.

How QuickFIR Works

  1. Fill out the report form with details about the incident
  2. Your report is sent securely to the nearest police station
  3. Connect with a lawyer for legal advice if needed
  4. An officer will review your case and contact you
  5. Follow up on your case through the provided contact information
